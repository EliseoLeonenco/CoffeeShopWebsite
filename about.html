Our History

At Baza Brew Coffee House, our rich history is steeped in passion and friendship. It all began with four visionary friends who shared a deep love for coffee and a burning desire to create something truly extraordinary. Drawing inspiration from their diverse backgrounds and cultures, they embarked on a journey to craft a unique coffee experience. The secret to our exceptional coffee lies in the meticulous selection of coffee beans, where we blend the finest beans with a touch of magic - the Cypriot blue flower. This rare mountain flower, known for its delicate aroma and enchanting beauty, infuses our coffee with a distinct character that sets us apart. It is this perfect combination of friendship, culture, and talent that has shaped Baza Brew Coffee House into the beloved establishment it is today.
